(Captain) Week 1

December 2nd, 2016 (Friday)
Hans Gregersen


Hello.

So, the first week is over. I did about as well as I expected, getting a lot less done than I'd planned.

According to Steam I played almost 22 hours of games this week. I think we've identified a bit of a problem of mine.

Despite this... frankly worrying amount of playtime, I did get 3 pages of the design document done, as well as 2 pages of the prototype outline. This honestly isn't a lot. The design document was essentially just copying over existing notes in a more readable format, and the outline only has a few basic elements fully described and ready to go.

While I did do a full 40 hours of other work, that's not a very good excuse for using 22 of my remaining 128 hours to game. If you take out my sleep, I still have 50 hours of time remaining. I have no idea what I was doing during those hours, but aside from probably 8 total hours spent on what I did get done, what's important is that the rest of that time was spent doing nothing productive (Trust me)

While I'm trying to make my design document fairly comprehensive, my prototype outline is mostly based on what barebones features I would require to get something playable yet also mostly representetive of my current vision. More complex features will be left until later on in development.

I'm going to get more specific with my goals this week, now that I know what I'm up for.

Goals for Week 2:
3 more pages of design document
4 more pages of prototype outline
Less than 22 hours spent playing games

Alright, I'll be back next week.

Hans
